What it is: Traditional Tamil sambar with finely chopped or pureed vegetables

Secret trick: Blend carrots, bottle gourd, and drumsticks into the dal - they won't even notice! 😉

Kid appeal: Serve with colorful rice or let them dip crispy dosa pieces!
